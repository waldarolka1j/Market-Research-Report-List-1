<p><p>The rollover car wash machine market is divided into three main types: frictional, contactless, and hybrid. </p><p>Frictional systems use brushes or cloths to scrub the vehicle’s surface, providing a thorough clean, though they may sometimes cause minor scratches. </p><p>Contactless washes utilize high-pressure water jets and specialized detergents, minimizing the risk of scratches while effectively cleaning surfaces. </p><p>Hybrid systems combine both methods, offering flexibility to cater to different customer preferences and vehicle conditions, maximizing efficiency and cleanliness.</p></p>
