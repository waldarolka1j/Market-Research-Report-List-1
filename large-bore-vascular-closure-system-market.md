<p><p>The Large Bore Vascular Closure System market consists of two primary types: active closure devices and passive closure devices. Active closure devices use mechanical or energy-driven methods to close vascular access sites, enabling faster hemostasis and more reliable outcomes. In contrast, passive closure devices rely on natural biological processes to achieve hemostasis without additional intervention. Both types cater to the needs of various medical procedures, addressing complications and enhancing patient safety in vascular access and closure.</p></p>
