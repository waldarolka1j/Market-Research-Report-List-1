<p><p>The plastic bonded magnets market comprises two key types: plastic injected magnets and plastic pressed magnets. Plastic injected magnets are produced using injection molding techniques, allowing for complex shapes and efficient mass production, widely used in automotive and electronics. In contrast, plastic pressed magnets are created through powder metallurgy, where magnet powder is combined with plastic and pressed into shape, often offering improved performance in specific applications. Both types benefit from lightweight properties and enhanced design flexibility.</p></p>
