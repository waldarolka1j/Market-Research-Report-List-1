<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing DAS System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing</li><li>Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) system market is primarily categorized into two types based on the type of acoustic wave used: Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing and Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing. Longitudinal wave sensing employs compressional waves traveling parallel to the sensing fiber, effective for applications like pipeline monitoring and geophysical surveys. In contrast, transverse wave sensing uses shear waves, providing superior sensitivity in detecting vibrations and disturbances, making it ideal for applications in safety monitoring and structural assessments.</p></p>
