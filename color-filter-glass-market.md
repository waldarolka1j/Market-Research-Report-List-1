<p><strong>The Color Filter Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longpass</li><li>Bandpass</li><li>Shortpass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color filter glass market can be categorized into three main types: longpass, bandpass, and shortpass filters. Longpass filters transmit wavelengths longer than a specified cutoff, effectively blocking shorter wavelengths. Bandpass filters allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass while blocking others outside this range. Shortpass filters, conversely, transmit shorter wavelengths and block longer ones. These filters are widely used in applications such as photography, spectroscopy, and optical instruments to enhance image quality and enable precise light manipulation.</p></p>
