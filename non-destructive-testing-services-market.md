<p><p>Non-destructive testing (NDT) services encompass various methods to evaluate the integrity of materials without causing damage. Ultrasonic testing utilizes high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws, while radiography testing employs X-rays or gamma rays to visualize internal structures. Magnetic particle testing identifies surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials using magnetic fields. Liquid penetrant testing reveals surface-breaking flaws through dye penetration. Other methods include visual inspection and eddy current testing, catering to diverse applications across industries like aerospace, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
