<p><p>The Surgical Camera System market comprises two primary types: Camera Heads and Camera Control Units (CCUs). Camera heads are high-definition devices equipped with advanced imaging capabilities, ensuring precise visualization during surgical procedures. They capture critical images and videos for documentation and analysis. CCUs, on the other hand, serve as the processing hub, managing and enhancing the video output from multiple camera sources. Together, they facilitate seamless communication and integration within the operating room, improving surgical outcomes and efficiency.</p></p>
