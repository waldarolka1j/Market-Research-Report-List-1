<p><strong>The Terahertz Imaging Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Terahertz Imaging Systems</li><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Terahertz imaging technology encompasses two primary market types: active and passive terahertz imaging systems. Active terahertz imaging systems generate their own terahertz radiation, providing high-resolution images for applications such as security screening and material characterization. In contrast, passive terahertz imaging systems detect naturally emitted terahertz radiation without needing an external source, making them ideal for non-invasive inspections and monitoring. Both types leverage the unique properties of terahertz waves to deliver detailed imaging in various fields, including medical, security, and telecommunications.</p></p>
