<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Hot Melt-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The label adhesives market comprises three main types: water-based, hot melt-based, and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives use water as a primary solvent, offering low VOC emissions and easy cleanup, ideal for environmentally conscious applications. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic polymers that provide quick bonding upon cooling, suitable for high-speed labeling processes. Solvent-based adhesives use organic solvents, ensuring strong adhesion and durability, making them suitable for challenging surfaces and conditions. Each type caters to specific labeling needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
