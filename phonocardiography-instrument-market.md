<p><p>The phonocardiography instrument market can be categorized into two primary types: wire type and wireless type. Wire type instruments rely on physical connections to transmit heart sounds to the recording device, ensuring stable and consistent signal quality. In contrast, wireless type instruments utilize Bluetooth or other wireless technologies to transmit data, offering greater mobility and convenience for healthcare providers. Both types are essential for monitoring heart conditions, but the choice depends on practical needs and technological preferences in clinical settings.</p></p>
