<p><p>The catalytic converter market primarily comprises two main types: two-way converters and three-way converters. Two-way converters focus on oxidizing carbon monoxide and unburned hydrocarbons, making them suitable for gasoline engines. In contrast, three-way converters handle oxidation and reduction, effectively managing nitrogen oxides along with hydrocarbons and carbon monoxide, thus supporting stricter emissions standards found in modern vehicles. Other market segments may include specialty converters for hybrid, electric vehicles, or heavy-duty applications, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
