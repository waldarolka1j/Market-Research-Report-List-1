<p><strong>The Waterproof Night Vision Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof night vision goggles market is primarily segmented into two types: image intensifier and thermal imaging devices. Image intensifier goggles amplify existing light, allowing users to see in low-light conditions by enhancing available ambient light. In contrast, thermal imaging goggles detect heat signatures, enabling visibility in complete darkness by transforming thermal energy into a visible image. Both technologies cater to diverse applications, including military, law enforcement, and outdoor activities, while the waterproof feature ensures durability in challenging environments.</p></p>
