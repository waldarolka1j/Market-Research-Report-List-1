<p><p>Carded packaging is a popular choice for various products, primarily categorized into thermoforming and cold forming types. Thermoforming involves heating plastic sheets and shaping them to create molded trays and blisters, offering durability and visibility for items. In contrast, cold forming uses pressure to shape materials at room temperature, which is ideal for creating tightly sealed, tamper-resistant packaging often used in pharmaceuticals. Both methods enhance product protection while providing appealing presentation and ease of handling for consumers.</p></p>
