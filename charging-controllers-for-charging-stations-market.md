<p><p>Charging controllers for charging stations come in two primary types: offline and online charger controllers. Offline charger controllers operate independently, managing the charging process without continuous connectivity to a central system. They are typically simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for basic charging needs. In contrast, online charger controllers maintain constant communication with a central server, enabling real-time monitoring, diagnostics, and updates. This type enhances user experience, supports dynamic pricing, and integrates seamlessly with smart grid technologies for improved energy management.</p></p>
