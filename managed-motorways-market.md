<p><strong>The Managed Motorways Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Controlled Motorways</li><li>Dynamic Hard Shoulder Running Schemes</li><li>All Lane Running Schemes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Managed motorways encompass three main market types designed to optimize traffic flow and reduce congestion. Controlled motorways utilize variable speed limits and lane restrictions to manage vehicle speed and density. Dynamic hard shoulder running schemes allow the hard shoulder to be opened as a traffic lane during peak times, enhancing capacity. All lane running schemes eliminate the hard shoulder entirely, using all lanes for traffic while implementing safety measures, improving throughput and reducing delays on busy routes.</p></p>
