<p><ul><li>Gel Strong Acid Cation Exchange Resin (SAC)</li><li>Macroporous Strong Acid Cation Exchange Resin (SAC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Strong Acid Cation Exchange Resin (SAC) market is divided into two main types: Gel SAC and Macroporous SAC. Gel SAC consists of uniform small pores and offers high exchange capacity, making it ideal for applications requiring fine ionic separation. On the other hand, Macroporous SAC features larger pores that allow for higher flow rates and better fouling resistance, suited for applications with higher ionic loads and varying conditions. Both types serve essential roles in water treatment and industrial processes.</p></p>
