<p><p>The molecular influenza diagnostic market includes several advanced technologies for detecting influenza viruses. Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR) is a highly sensitive method used to convert RNA to DNA, enabling precise virus detection. Nucleic Acid Sequence Based Amplification (NASBA) offers rapid amplification of RNA without thermal cycling. Loop-mediated Isothermal Amplification (LAMP) allows for quick results under isothermal conditions. Simple Amplification-based Assays (SAMBA) provide a user-friendly approach for diagnostic testing, facilitating timely and accurate influenza detection in clinical settings.</p></p>
