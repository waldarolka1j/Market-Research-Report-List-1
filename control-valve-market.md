<p><p>The control valve market comprises three primary types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electrical control valves. Pneumatic control valves utilize compressed air for operation, offering rapid response and suitability for various industrial applications. Hydraulic control valves leverage fluid pressure to control motion and flow, ideal for high-power operations in manufacturing and heavy machinery. Electrical control valves operate using electrical signals to regulate flow, known for precision and integration with automation systems. Each type serves distinct needs in process automation and control across industries.</p></p>
