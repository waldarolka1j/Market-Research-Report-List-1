<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cyanide Ion Selective Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystal Membrane</li><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cyanide ion selective electrodes market is categorized into three main types: crystal membrane, liquid membrane, and PVC membrane electrodes. Crystal membrane electrodes utilize stable crystalline structures for detecting cyanide ions, providing high selectivity and sensitivity. Liquid membrane electrodes leverage a liquid phase to selectively allow cyanide ions to pass through while blocking others, offering enhanced performance. PVC membrane electrodes incorporate polyvinyl chloride to form a flexible and durable sensing layer, though they may have slightly lower sensitivity than crystal and liquid variants. Each type serves specific applications in environmental monitoring and industrial processes.</p></p>
