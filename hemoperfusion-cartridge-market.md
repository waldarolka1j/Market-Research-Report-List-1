<p><p>The hemoperfusion cartridge market consists primarily of two types: charcoal hemoperfusion and certain resins hemoperfusion. In contrast, certain resins hemoperfusion employs synthetic resins designed to selectively bind specific toxins, offering targeted treatment for conditions like renal failure and liver dysfunction. Both types enhance detoxification processes in patients, contributing to improved clinical outcomes.</p></p>
