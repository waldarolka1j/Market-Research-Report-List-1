<p><p>The Tahini sauce market is primarily divided into two types: hulled and unhulled tahini. Hulled tahini is made from sesame seeds with their hulls removed, resulting in a smoother texture and milder flavor. It is often preferred for its versatility in various recipes. Unhulled tahini, on the other hand, retains the hulls, providing a nuttier taste and higher nutritional value due to the inclusion of more fiber and minerals. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and culinary applications.</p></p>
