<p><p>The blood clot retrieval devices market encompasses various types designed to remove thrombi from blood vessels. Mechanical embolus removal devices utilize suction or mechanical disruption to extract clots. Penumbra systems combine suction and aspiration technology for effective retrieval. Stent retrievers deploy mesh-like structures to capture and remove thrombi. Ultrasound-assisted devices enhance clot disintegration through ultrasonic energy, improving the effectiveness of retrieval. Together, these devices play a vital role in treating ischemic strokes and enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
