<p><p>Base epoxy resins are categorized into two primary types: glycidyl epoxy and non-glycidyl epoxy. Glycidyl epoxy resins are characterized by their high reactivity, excellent adhesion, and durability, making them suitable for applications like coatings, adhesives, and electronics. Non-glycidyl epoxy resins, while generally having lower reactivity, offer enhanced flexibility, chemical resistance, and lower viscosity, making them ideal for various industrial applications, including composites and structural materials. Each type serves specific needs in the materials market.</p></p>
