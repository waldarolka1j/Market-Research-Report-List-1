<p><p>The commercial display cases market consists of three main types: normal temperature, chilling, and frozen types. Chilling display cases maintain a cool environment for perishable items such as dairy and fresh produce, enhancing their shelf life. Frozen display cases operate at sub-zero temperatures, ideal for storing ice cream, frozen meals, and other frozen products. Each type caters to specific storage needs, influencing their design and application in retail environments.</p></p>
