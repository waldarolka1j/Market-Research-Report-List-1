<p><strong>The Plastic Ball Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Connections</li><li>Flanged Connections</li><li>Butt Weld Connections</li><li>Socked Weld Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic ball valves market is categorized by connection types: </p><p>1. **Threaded Connections** allow easy assembly and disassembly for maintenance.  </p><p>2. **Flanged Connections** provide a robust seal and are ideal for large pipes.  </p><p>3. **Butt Weld Connections** offer a permanent and leak-free solution, suitable for high-pressure applications.  </p><p>4. **Socket Weld Connections** allow pipes to be connected at right angles, minimizing turbulence, and are typically used in smaller diameter systems. Each type serves specific applications based on installation needs and pressure requirements.</p></p>
