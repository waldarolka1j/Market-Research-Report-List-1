<p><strong>The Elastography Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasound</li><li>Magnetic Resonance Elastography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elastography imaging encompasses two primary types: Ultrasound Elastography and Magnetic Resonance Elastography (MRE). Ultrasound Elastography utilizes high-frequency sound waves to assess tissue elasticity, commonly used to evaluate liver stiffness and detect tumors. In contrast, Magnetic Resonance Elastography employs MRI technology to visualize and measure the mechanical properties of tissues, providing detailed insights into soft tissue stiffness, especially in the brain and liver. Both modalities enhance diagnostic accuracy and aid in the monitoring of various medical conditions.</p></p>
