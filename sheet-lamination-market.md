<p><strong>The Sheet Lamination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Additive Manufacturing (UAM)</li><li>Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sheet lamination encompasses two primary market types: Ultrasonic Additive Manufacturing (UAM) and Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM). UAM utilizes ultrasonic waves to bond metal sheets, allowing for intricate metal parts with minimal waste, often in aerospace and automotive applications. Conversely, LOM employs layers of adhesive-coated sheets, typically paper, to build up 3D models, commonly used in prototyping and educational environments. Both methods offer unique advantages in material efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and the versatility of applications in various industries.</p></p>
