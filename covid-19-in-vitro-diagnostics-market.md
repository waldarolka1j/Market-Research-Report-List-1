<p><strong>The COVID-19 In-Vitro Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 in-vitro diagnostics market includes various testing methods. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results through lateral flow assays, ideal for on-site testing. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) are lab-based tests that detect antibodies or antigens with high sensitivity and specificity, suitable for large-scale screening. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, critical for evaluating vaccine efficacy and immune response. Each method plays a vital role in managing the pandemic and ensuring public health safety.</p></p>
