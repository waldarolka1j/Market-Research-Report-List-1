<p><ul><li>GCC</li><li>PCC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic calcium carbonate market is primarily segmented into two types: Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC). GCC is derived from natural limestone through physical processes, offering a cost-effective solution for various applications like construction and plastics. In contrast, PCC is produced through chemical processes, resulting in a finer, purer product tailored for specialized uses, including pharmaceuticals and food. Each type serves distinct industries, contributing to the overall growth and diversification of the calcium carbonate market.</p></p>
