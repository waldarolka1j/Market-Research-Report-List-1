<p><ul><li>Diagnostic</li><li>Prognostic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated Vehicle Health Management (IVHM) focuses on monitoring and optimizing vehicle performance through two primary market types: diagnostic and prognostic systems. Diagnostic systems analyze real-time data to identify existing issues within vehicle components, enabling timely maintenance and repairs. Prognostic systems, on the other hand, utilize predictive analytics to forecast potential failures before they occur, allowing proactive interventions. Together, these systems enhance vehicle reliability, reduce downtime, and improve overall operational efficiency, making them essential in modern automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
